Introduction

The Internet is a very popular place. As of 2014, the number of active web-sites has passed 1 billion, and the global Internet-using population has surpassed 2.4 billion. Every minute, Google receives over 4 million search requests. However, despite its popularity, few Internet users actually understand the "inner workings" of web development.

But since you are in this club, you will understand the concepts of web development, and eventually, you will create your own web-site.



Why Learn Web Development

Information and communications systems have become integral to our modern technological society, and programming skills attract increasing demand in industry and business

There are many reasons for learning web development, and here are just a few:

  1. Influence
    • You can spread your influence to millions of people around the world
    • You can present and publish your ideas to a wider audience
    • You can create an online startup and market web services
  2. Respect
    • You will gain confidence
    • You will develop your programming skills
  3. Personal Satisfaction
    • You can create cool stuff
    • You can show Off
    • YOU WILL HAVE FUN


Course Outline


Throughout the first half of the year, we will focus on the fundamentals of web-page design.

Mostly, this will involve learning HTML and CSS languages.

In the second half of the year, we will focus on more advanced web languages that you can use to create awesome features.

Mostly, this will involve learning languages like Javascript and PHP.

Also, you will learn how to host your own web-sites and web-apps.

Besides learning the concepts, we will experiment in writing code.

There is no homework, but we will recommend weekly activities or projects for your to work on.

Students are free to work together or chill out.



Extra Info


This will be a laid-back club. We will not move too fast, and we want you to relax and have fun.

There will be a point system in the club.

- Points will be rewarded for participation

- Also, they will be rewarded for good code or amazing ideas.

- You can get awesome and delicious prizes if you win enough points.
